Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and disclose your personal information when you visit, use, make a purchase or otherwise communicate with us. If there is a conflict between our Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, this Privacy Policy controls regarding personal information.

Please read this policy carefully. By using the Services, you acknowledge that you understand the collection, use and disclosure described here.

01

Personal Information We Collect or Process

“Personal information” means information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to you. It does not include anonymous or de-identified information.

  • Contact details such as your name, billing and shipping address, phone number and email.
  • Financial and payment information, transaction details and payment confirmation.
  • Account details, preferences and settings.
  • Items you view, place in your cart or wishlist, purchase, return, exchange or cancel.
  • Customer-support communications, device information, IP address and usage information.
02

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, use the Services or contact us; automatically from your device through cookies and similar technologies; from service providers acting on our behalf; and from partners or other third parties.

We may also use it for marketing and advertising, security and fraud prevention, legal compliance, valid government requests, litigation and enforcement of our terms and policies.
