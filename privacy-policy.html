Information we collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email, delivery and billing address, order history, support messages and device or website usage information. Payments are processed through payment providers; we do not store complete card details.

How we use it

Sharing & retention

We share only necessary information with delivery partners, payment providers and service vendors working on our behalf. We do not sell your personal information. Records are retained only as long as reasonably required for service and legal purposes.
