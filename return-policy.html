01

Return Eligibility

You may request a return within 7 days if the product arrived damaged, was the wrong item or has a manufacturing defect.

  • The item must be unused and in its original condition.
  • All original tags, labels and accessories must be included.
  • The item must be in its original packaging with valid proof of purchase.

Returns that do not meet these conditions may not be accepted.

02

How to Request a Return

Contact our support team within 7 days at support@giraastore.com. Include your order number, customer name, reason for return and clear product photographs where relevant.

We normally respond within 1–2 business days. Do not send products back before receiving approval.

03

Return Shipping

For an approved damaged, incorrect or defective product, Giraastore will provide return instructions and may arrange shipment. For other approved reasons, return shipping charges may be the customer’s responsibility.

04

Non-Returnable Items

  • Used products, products damaged after delivery or items not in original condition.
  • Items without original packaging, tags or accessories.
  • Personalised or customised products and gift cards.
  • Clearance or sale items marked “Final Sale”.
  • Products specifically marked non-returnable.
05

Exchanges

Exchanges are offered for damaged products, wrong products or manufacturing defects and remain subject to availability. If the requested item is unavailable, we may offer a refund or store credit.

06

Refund Process

After receiving and inspecting the item, we will notify you whether the refund is approved. Approved refunds are sent to the original payment method, generally within 5–10 business days. Bank or provider processing time may vary.

Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return is caused by our error.

07

Order Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled only before they are packed or shipped. Dispatched orders cannot be cancelled. Contact us immediately if you need to request cancellation.

08

Damaged or Incorrect Products

Inspect your order upon delivery. Report damaged, defective or incorrect items, or missing parts, within 48 hours with clear photographs and order details. We will investigate and provide an appropriate resolution.

09

Refund Rejection

A request may be declined when it is submitted after the eligible period, the product shows use or customer-caused damage, packaging or accessories are missing, or the product is non-returnable.

10

Changes to This Policy

Giraastore may update this Return & Refund Policy at any time. Changes become effective when published. Please review this policy periodically.
