7-day return window

You may request a return within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed and returned with their original packaging, tags, accessories and invoice.

Damaged or incorrect products

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number, clear photographs and an unedited unboxing video. After review, we may arrange a replacement, store credit or refund.

Non-returnable items

  • Personalised, made-to-order or clearance products
  • Items showing use, wear, washing or missing packaging
  • Hygiene-sensitive products where the seal has been opened
  • Minor colour differences caused by screen settings

Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after inspection. COD refunds may be made by bank transfer or UPI. Original shipping and COD charges are not refundable unless we made an error.
