Order processing

Orders are normally packed within 1–2 business days. During launches, sales or public holidays, processing may take slightly longer. You will receive an update when your parcel is dispatched.

Delivery times

Standard delivery across India usually takes 3–7 business days after dispatch. Remote locations may require additional time. Delivery estimates are not guaranteed because weather, carrier delays and local restrictions can affect transit.

Shipping charges

Standard shipping charges are shown at checkout. We offer free standard shipping on eligible prepaid orders above ₹499.

Tracking & address changes

Tracking details are shared by email or SMS after dispatch. Please check your complete address and phone number before placing an order. Address changes are possible only before packing; call +91 84249 13989 promptly.

If tracking has not updated for 72 hours, contact us with your order number and we will help.
