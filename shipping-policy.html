1. Processing Time

All orders are normally processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders are not shipped or delivered on Sundays or public holidays.

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

We deliver across India using our trusted courier partners.

  • Standard Shipping: 3–7 business days
  • Express Shipping, where available: 1–3 business days

Delivery timelines may vary depending on your location and courier service.

3. Shipping Charges

We offer FREE SHIPPING on eligible prepaid orders above ₹499. For Cash on Delivery orders, any applicable shipping or COD fee will be displayed at checkout.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link through SMS, email or WhatsApp so you can track your shipment.

5. Packaging

We pack every order with love and care to ensure your products reach you safely and in perfect condition.

6. Delays

While we try our best to deliver on time, delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather, courier issues or holidays. We appreciate your patience and support.
